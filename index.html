Homemade Italian Pizza

Make perfect pizza at home with this classic homemade pizza recipe, including a pizza dough recipe, topping suggestions, and step-by-step instructions with photos.

By Elise Bauer

Prep Time: 2 hrs Servings: 4 to 6 Cook Time: 30 mins


Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 cups (355 ml) warm water (105°F-115°F)
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 3 3/4 cups (490g) bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil (omit if cooking pizza in a wood-fired pizza oven)
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
Method
  1. Proof the yeast
  2. Make and knead the pizza dough
  3. Let the dough rise
  4. Preheat the pizza stone (or pizza pan or baking sheet)
  5. Divide the dough into two balls
  6. Prep the toppings
  7. Flatten the dough ball, and stretch out into a round
  8. Brush the dough top with olive oil
  9. Sprinkle the pizza peel with corn meal, put flattened dough on top
  10. Spread with tomato sauce and sprinkle with toppings
  11. Sprinkle the cornmeal on pizza stone, slide pizza onto pizza stone in oven
  12. Bake

Enjoy..
